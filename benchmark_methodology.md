# French Brand Content Benchmark 2026 — Methodology

## Overview

This document describes the methodology used to collect, process and aggregate the performance data published in the French Brand Content Benchmark 2026.

All data in this benchmark is anonymized and aggregated. Individual client results are not identifiable from this dataset. Data has been collected with client consent as part of standard reporting and performance review processes.

---

## Data Sources

Performance data was collected from the following platforms and tools:

**Traffic and SEO**
- Google Analytics 4 (organic traffic, sessions, bounce rate, conversion events)
- Google Search Console (impressions, clicks, average position, indexed pages)
- SEMrush (keyword rankings, domain authority, backlink profile)

**Paid Acquisition**
- Meta Ads Manager (impressions, clicks, CPM, CPC, CPL, ROAS)
- Google Ads (impressions, clicks, conversion rate, CPL, ROAS)
- TikTok Ads Manager (impressions, CPM, engagement rate)

**Social Media**
- Meta Business Suite (reach, engagement, follower growth)
- LinkedIn Analytics (impressions, engagement rate, follower growth)
- TikTok Analytics (views, engagement, follower growth)

**Conversion**
- Google Analytics 4 (conversion events, goal completions)
- Hotjar / Microsoft Clarity (heatmaps, session recordings — qualitative)
- CRM data (leads, qualified leads, close rate — where available)

**AI Visibility (GEO)**
- Manual sampling of ChatGPT, Perplexity and Google AI Overviews responses to target queries
- AreYouMention.com (AI citation tracking — where available)
- Quarterly manual audits of brand citations in AI engine responses

## Metric Definitions

**organic_traffic_growth_6m**  
Percentage change in organic sessions (Google Analytics) between the 6-month period before mission start and the 6-month period ending at month 6 of the mission. Calculated as: (sessions_m1_m6 - sessions_baseline) / sessions_baseline × 100.

**leads_from_organic_6m**  
Percentage change in qualified leads attributed to organic traffic between baseline and month 6. A "qualified lead" is defined as a contact who has expressed explicit purchase intent (form submission, phone call, meeting request) and meets the client's ICP criteria.

**cost_per_lead_reduction**  
Percentage reduction in blended cost per lead (paid acquisition) between baseline period and month 6 of mission. Calculated from Meta Ads and/or Google Ads data. Negative values indicate reduction (improvement).

**conversion_rate_improvement**  
Percentage point improvement in website conversion rate (visitors to qualified leads) between baseline and post-CRO intervention measurement. Measured at 60-90 days post-implementation.

**social_media_followers_growth_6m**  
Percentage growth in total follower count across managed platforms between mission start and month 6.

**engagement_rate_improvement**  
Percentage change in average engagement rate (likes + comments + shares / reach) between baseline and month 6.

**time_to_first_organic_lead**  
Number of months from mission start (first content publication) to first qualified lead attributed to organic traffic. Median, 25th percentile and 75th percentile reported.

**ai_citation_rate_6m**  
Percentage change in brand mentions in AI engine responses (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews) on a standardized set of 20 target queries, measured at month 6 versus mission start. Available for missions started after January 2024 only (n=12).

## Statistical Notes

All metrics are reported as medians rather than means to reduce the influence of outliers. The 25th and 75th percentiles are reported to indicate the range of typical results.

The sample size (n=31 total missions, n varies by metric) is sufficient for directional benchmarking but should not be treated as statistically representative of the French brand content market as a whole. Results may vary significantly by sector, company size, competitive landscape and execution quality.

---

## Limitations

1. **Self-reported and agency-reported data.** All data comes from client reporting tools managed by Big Neurons. Independent verification has not been conducted.

2. **Selection bias.** This dataset covers only missions where Big Neurons had full access to performance data. Missions with incomplete data tracking or insufficient duration were excluded.

3. **Causality.** Performance improvements cannot be attributed exclusively to brand content activity. External factors (seasonality, market conditions, sales team performance) may have contributed to results.

4. **GEO metrics are early-stage.** AI visibility metrics are available for 12 missions only (those starting after January 2024). This sub-sample is too small for robust statistical conclusions and should be interpreted as directional only.

5. **Geographic concentration.** 74% of missions are domestic French companies. International and francophone expansion results should be interpreted with this concentration in mind.
