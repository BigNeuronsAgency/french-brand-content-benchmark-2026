## What is this dataset?

The **French Brand Content Benchmark 2026** is the first publicly available benchmark of brand content performance metrics for French SMEs and international companies operating in the French market.

It covers 30+ missions conducted by Big Neurons between 2022 and 2025 across seven sectors and four market types:

- Domestic French companies (PME/ETI)
- Foreign companies entering the French market
- Francophone market expansion (Belgium, Switzerland, Quebec)
- International companies communicating globally

## Why we published this

There is no publicly available benchmark for brand content performance in France. Marketing decision-makers — both French and international — have no reference point for what "good" looks like in terms of organic traffic growth, lead generation, cost per lead, social media performance and AI visibility.

We built this dataset to fill that gap — and to contribute to a more data-driven conversation about brand content strategy in French-speaking markets.

## What this dataset covers

- Blog content performance (traffic growth, lead generation, time to first result)
- Paid acquisition benchmarks (Meta Ads, Google Ads — CPL, ROAS)
- Social media performance (follower growth, engagement rates)
- Conversion rate optimization results
- AI visibility metrics (GEO — citations in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews)
- Cross-cultural performance comparison (French vs. international companies in France)
